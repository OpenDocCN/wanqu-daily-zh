# 有趣的市场:2019 年版-由 Elad Gil - Elad 博客

> 原文：<http://blog.eladgil.com/2019/01/interesting-markets-2019-edition.html?utm_source=wanqu.co&utm_campaign=Wanqu+Daily&utm_medium=website>

每年我都会后退一步，问一问技术领域是否有共同的主题。有时，这造成了一种非常向后看的观点，因为最明显的信号出现在几年来一直表现良好的公司中，这种趋势真的结束了。其他时候，信号被证明是错误的或推测性的。五六年前，许多人可能认为消费者物联网将由初创公司驱动，而事实上，大公司是大多数(但不是所有)消费者物联网应用的关键。

以下是我认为 2019 年特别耐人寻味的一些领域:

**1。Devsumer*产品。** *又名软件吃埃森哲。过去的一波技术创新是技术专业消费市场的兴起，或者说是“专业消费者”市场。我们现在看到了开发商&消费者市场或“devsumer”的新合并。

如果你是一家科技公司的运营或业务团队，或者基本上是一家非科技公司的任何人，很难获得任何工程资源来帮助构建成功所需的工作流程或生产力工具。公司将花费几十万，甚至几亿美元，雇佣世界各地的精英 来构建简单的应用程序和工具，或者为它们执行 API 集成。

并行地，一般的知识工作者已经变得更有悟性和动手能力。更多的人是在家里有电脑的情况下长大的， [在大学里上过 CS 课](https://www.thedp.com/article/2017/04/cis-coding-intro) (现在是许多大学最受欢迎的专业)，或者去了编码训练营或学校，如 [Lambda 学校](https://lambdaschool.com/) 。虽然不是所有接触 CS 的人最终都会成为成熟的开发人员，但一般来说，人们对软件元素和简单代码更有悟性。如果有简单的工具，人们会更愿意建造东西。

正如之前作为 IT productivity 1.0 一部分的 prosumer 浪潮一样，我们正在经历一个新的“devsumer”浪潮，消费者更有可能接受更轻量级的类似开发者的工作和产品。

出现了许多产品，这些产品允许人们构建简单的软件应用程序，或者使用模板化的应用程序来实现自己的工作流程或工作效率。你可以认为这是一个 SQL 数据库或 excel 电子表格，并把它变成一个应用程序平台。这些公司包括 [Airtable](https://airtable.com/) 以及以垂直方式具体到内部工具的 [Retool](https://tryretool.com/) 。

在不同的细分市场中， [概念](https://www.notion.so/Getting-Started-ad338cf8ddb04651a052710ff7c6dc46) 和 [Coda](https://coda.io/) 专注于 docs生产力软件的未来，而其他公司，如 [Zapier](https://zapier.com/apps) 和 [IFTTT](https://ifttt.com/) 允许您简单地将 API 缝合到工作流中。有一句老话，钱是通过捆绑或拆分产品或服务赚来的。Devsumer 公司实际上是将不同的生产力工具重新捆绑到一个公司中。* 

通过允许任何人使用现成的模板或工具，或者通过拼接现有的应用程序来创建他们自己的应用程序，编程的力量突然对企业或 SMB 中的任何人都可用。这些工具也可以用于个人生产力或企业生产力。

Devsumer 产品受到多种大趋势的驱动，包括整体转向基于云的应用程序和平台，导致可访问的集成 API，以及更精明的白领员工，他们希望软件工具成为他们工作和个人生活中不可或缺的一部分。

这个市场将会出现多个 50 亿美元以上的公司(Airtable 已经以11 亿美元的估值进行了一轮融资)。
 **2。房地产技术。** *软件吃房地产。世界上最大的资产之一是房地产，个人净资产的很大一部分与他们的房子有关。从某种意义上说， [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.com/) 是最早的房地产技术颠覆者之一，因为它允许人们通过出租给别人来变现他们最昂贵的资产。

以 [Opendoor](https://www.opendoor.com/) 为先锋的新一波房地产科技公司已经出现。房地产技术公司正在试验部分所有权( [Divvy](https://www.divvyhomes.com/) 、 [Harbor](https://harbor.com/) )、建造房屋和街区的新方法( [Mosaic](http://mosaic-builders.com/) 、 [Katerra](https://www.katerra.com/) )以及寻找出租房屋的新方法( [平房](https://bungalow.com/) )。还有多种房屋购买服务最终转移到了网上——例如针对房地产交易的或以线上线下混合模式购买当地房地产经纪公司的 [Compass](https://www.compass.com/) 。* 

 **3。机器学习/人工智能硅&系统。** *每一次重大技术转变都会创造出一家价值 200 亿美元以上的半导体公司。*
大多数主要的技术浪潮都会催生一家价值超过 100 亿美元的新半导体公司。例如，微型计算机的兴起导致了 [英特尔](https://newsroom.intel.com/) 和 AMD，联网导致了 [博通](https://www.broadcom.com/) ，图形处理和视频游戏导致了 [英伟达](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/) ，而移动技术则导致了 [【高通】](https://www.qualcomm.com/) 和 ARM。

机器学习/ AI 是新的技术浪潮，有自己的硅&硬件需求。虽然 NVIDIA GPUs 目前被许多人用于机器学习，但底层硬件并没有针对快速 I/O 和矩阵乘法进行优化，而快速 I/O 和矩阵乘法会针对机器学习进行优化。谷歌为 ML 推出了名为 [TPUs](https://cloud.google.com/tpu/) (张量处理单元)的定制 ASICs，但谷歌不愿意对外销售硬件——部分是为了给自己的云服务带来竞争优势。这一点，加上在自动驾驶汽车(自动驾驶汽车，无人机等)中运行的潜在无处不在的人工智能模型的潜在中期增长。)产生了巨大的市场机会**。

**4。运输和物流。** *软件吃运输、物流和供应链。*
实物商品在世界各地流动的方式正在发生变化。整个运输和物流栈正在被软件蚕食。自动驾驶汽车的基础层离主流还有几年的时间，但是已经有了有希望的早期信号。像[Embark](https://embarktrucks.com/)[kod iak](http://www.kodiak.ai/)[Starsky](https://www.starsky.io/)等公司都在打造自动驾驶卡车。除此之外，像 [车队](https://convoy.com/) 和 [优步](https://www.uberfreight.com/) 这样的公司正在为卡车运输建立装载市场和物流网络，而 [KeepTruckin](https://keeptruckin.com/) 和 [轮回](https://www.samsara.com/) 则负责车队管理和合规。像 [Flexport](https://www.flexport.com/) 这样的公司正在推动全球货运。新的机器人公司正在尝试自动化仓库，并允许小型城市配送中心( [常识机器人](https://www.cs-robotics.com/) )。最后，像 [Shippo](https://goshippo.com/) 这样的公司帮助最终用户的运输过程。 

**5\. Crypto. ***Programmable money and Store of Value.*
Cryptocurrencies and the underlying set of companies creating the new financial stack promise to be incredibly valuable. Bitcoin and Ethereum still hold immense promise as new forms of programmable money / store of value, in part due to their resistance to seizure and censorship. Despite the crypto winter, new protocols like Chia, Celo, Coda, Oasis, and Thunder are looking to re-invent compute and money while protocols like [DIRT](https://dirtprotocol.com/) ask how to move data onto the blockchain. While a subset of these approaches will undoubtedly fail, there is immense room for innovation.

In parallel, a new financial stack is being build to to trade, custody, and derivative crypto in the form of [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/), [Anchorage](https://anchorage.com/), and [dYdX](https://dydx.exchange/) with Coinbase acting as one of the very regulatory compliant fiat/crypto on ramps. In parallel, efforts to create new ways to securitize hard assets (Harbor) or for capital formation ([Coinlist](https://coinlist.co/)) are driving other aspects of the early market forward.

I am still quite bullish that a basked of cryptocurrencies will be worth in the trillions within the next 10 years. The primary short term driver for crypto value with be the generational replacement of gold as a store-of-value for a specific subset of the population. The longer term drivers of crypto value will be new forms of programable money and on-chain securities.
**6\. TBD: Legal tech.** *Software eating law firms.*
While more nascent, a new wave of companies are focused on eating away at the legal world. [Atrium](https://www.atrium.co/) is the world's first tech-driven law firm, while companies like [Klarity](https://www.klaritylaw.com/) apply machine learning to legal contracts. In parallel, [Stripe's Atlas](https://stripe.com/atlas) allows companies to incorporate via API. [Carta](https://carta.com/) provides cap table management as a basis to branch into other services. It is early days in legal tech but it is possible these early seeds will form deep roots.

Given the degree to which legal services have a series of highly repetitive tasks that lawyers and para-legals would prefer not to do, it is surprising more software has not come to this vertical.
**7\. TBD: Defense?** *Big company employee politics creating a market opportunity.*
Silicon Valley has its roots in the defense industry. A good overview may be found [with Steve Blank](https://steveblank.com/secret-history/). The recent Trump election helped to further radicalize US politics and this political divide has found its way Silicon Valley as well. While a strong national defense matters for the USA irrespective of who the president is, it is suddenly unpopular at a number of companies to accept defense related work. Google shut down [project Maven](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/01/technology/google-pentagon-project-maven.html) which met with a negative employee response, and employees have protested defense contractors at Microsoft, Google, Amazon and others.

The rise of the radicalized worker has created an opportunity for startups to pursue defense work. The defense and intelligence community wants to work with major brands like Google. However, if Google is unwilling to lend its AI expertise, for example, to assess defense video feeds, startups will emerge take on this work.

To date, [Anduril](https://www.anduril.com/) may be the defense company with the most interest although others like [Shield.AI](http://shield.ai/) are also in the market. There is an opportunity here to build a next-generation defense company.

**Summary**
In sum, there are 5 core markets (Devsumer, Real Estate, AI hardware, Transportation and Logistics, Crypto) and 2 emerging ones (Legal, Defense) that seem quite exciting to watch in 2019\. While there is a potential darkening macro economic environment, software continues to eat the world and to remake it.
